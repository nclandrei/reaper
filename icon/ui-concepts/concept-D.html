Resource Heatmap

A thermal-camera inspired interface where every process is a heat tile sized by resource consumption and colored by threat level. No lists, no tables — just an instant visual readout of system health. Dominant processes blaze red and large; idle ones shrink to cool blue specks. Hover to inspect, click to kill.
