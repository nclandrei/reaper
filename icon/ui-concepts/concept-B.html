Concept B

Hit List

A bounty-board approach to process management. Processes are ranked by a composite threat score that combines CPU and memory usage, surfacing the worst offenders as "Most Wanted" targets with prominent kill actions. The tactical HUD aesthetic makes resource reclamation feel decisive and satisfying rather than administrative.
